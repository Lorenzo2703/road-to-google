# 📝 STAR Story Bank Template

> Write 8-10 STAR stories covering all Google evaluation dimensions.
> Practice telling each in 2-3 minutes.

---

## Story Template

```
**Tags**: `tag1` `tag2` `tag3`

**Situation** (2-3 sentences): Context — company, team, project

**Task** (1-2 sentences): YOUR specific responsibility

**Action** (4-6 bullets): What YOU did — be specific

**Result** (2-3 sentences): Quantified outcome + learning

**Questions This Answers**: List 2-3 behavioral questions
```
